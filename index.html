How Monthly Peer Lending Works

A simple 4-step cycle that repeats every month.

1

Members Contribute

Each member contributes a fixed monthly amount to a shared lending pool.

2

Requests Open

Workers request loans for urgent needs like rent, healthcare, or transportation.

3

Loans Are Issued

Community-approved loans are sent out based on circle rules and available funds.

4

Repayments Recycle

Repayments return to the pool so the next members can borrow the following month.
